Privacy Policy

Last updated · July 11, 2026

This page explains what the Archive collects, why, and what control you have over it. It is written to be read, not skimmed past.

01 Overview

ManuscriptZero ("the Archive") is a private literary archive operated by Void Stanza. This policy governs what information is collected when you access the Archive, how it is used, and the rights available to you regarding it.

02 Information We Collect

  • Access data. A session marker is stored locally on your device after you enter the correct passphrase. It confirms authorization to view the vault and clears automatically when your browser session ends.
  • Reading progress. Stored on your device to let you resume where you left off. This does not currently require an account or login.
  • Comments (Marginalia). If you submit a comment, we store the comment text and the display name you choose. Email addresses are not collected through the comment form.
  • Technical and security data. To prevent spam and abuse, the Archive may log technical identifiers, such as an IP address, associated with blocked or flagged submissions.
  • Analytics. We use Umami, a privacy-focused, cookieless analytics service to collect aggregated information such as page views, referring sites, and general browser or device type. No advertising profiles are created.

03 How We Use Information

Information is used strictly to operate the Archive: authenticating access, preserving reading progress, moderating comments, preventing spam, and understanding how the site is used so it can be improved. Information is never used for advertising, and it is never sold or rented to any third party.

04 No Payment Information

The Archive does not process payments and collects no financial information of any kind.

05 Third-Party Services

The Archive relies on the following providers to operate. Each processes only what is required to perform its function, and each maintains its own independent privacy practices:

  • GitHub Pages — website hosting
  • Supabase — database, comment storage, and moderation
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Umami — privacy-focused analytics
  • Google Fonts — font delivery

06 Cookies & Local Storage

The Archive does not use cookies. It uses your browser's sessionStorage to hold a passphrase-verified session; this data lives only in your browser and clears when the tab or browser closes.

Some third-party services may use their own technologies necessary to deliver their services. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details.

07 Data Retention

Comments are retained until removed by the Archive or by request. Reading progress persists on-device until cleared. Security logs are retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to detect, investigate, or prevent abuse.

08 Your Rights

Where applicable under relevant privacy laws, you may request access to, correction of, deletion of, or restriction of personal data held about you — such as a submitted comment — through the contact details below.

09 Security

Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards are used to protect information held by the Archive. However, no method of transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

10 Children's Privacy

The Archive is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required by applicable law) and does not knowingly collect personal information from them.

11 International Visitors

The Archive is operated from India. If you access it from another country, your information may be processed in jurisdictions whose data protection laws differ from those of your own.

12 Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the Archive after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
