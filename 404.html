ManuscriptZero — Community Guidelines
Before you read & write
I. This is a literary space. ManuscriptZero exists for fiction, craft, and honest response to the written word. Keep all engagement anchored to the work.
II. Speak with care. Criticism is welcome; cruelty is not. Challenge ideas, not people. Comments that exist only to diminish will not be approved.
III. No hate, harassment, or harm. Content targeting anyone on the basis of identity — race, gender, religion, sexuality, or otherwise — has no place here and will be permanently removed.
IV. Spoilers carry weight. If your comment reveals plot, mark it clearly. Not everyone reads at the same pace.
V. Spam and self-promotion are not commentary. Links, advertisements, and off-topic solicitations will be rejected without notice.
VI. Moderation is final. All comments are reviewed before they appear. The author reserves the right to approve, reject, or remove any submission at their sole discretion.
VII. Your words are yours — and visible. Once approved, comments are public. Do not post anything you would not wish to be read by anyone.
These guidelines exist not to restrict, but to protect the integrity of this archive and everyone who enters it. ManuscriptZero is a private literary space — treat it accordingly.
