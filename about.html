Hi, I'm Void Stanza.

That raven up there? Yeah — raven, not a crow. I will die on this hill. 🫠 That's me. I clean up pretty well.

But let's go a layer deeper than the feathers.

I'm the kind of creature who gets stuck on the unsaid — the glance someone didn't return, the sentence left unfinished, the feeling that never got a name.

This corner of the internet won't stay in one genre for long. Come along while I figure out my writing style and follow whichever story insists on being told.

Every character's name and every odd little detail is there for a reason, so use the Marginalia. Leave your theories — I genuinely want to hear them.

P.S. Novel, manhwa, and comic recs are always welcome.
