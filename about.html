Colophon

ManuscriptZero is a private literary archive — built, not templated, one page at a time, for a small and specific readership rather than an audience. The raven at the door is not a mascot. It watches, it remembers, and it does not explain itself more than it has to.

The seven records held here share a single preoccupation: the cost of not paying attention to people. The site tries, in its own small way, to practice what it publishes — restraint over ornament, implication over statement.
